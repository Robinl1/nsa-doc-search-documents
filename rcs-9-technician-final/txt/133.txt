-Battery event

Purpose
The Battery event triggers an action when the battery charge level is within the specified range.

‘ Tip: to reduce impact on battery use, it is best to link the Battery event, set between 0%-
30%, to Start and Stop Crisis actions. This way, if the battery charge level drops under the
set value, the agent's activities that consume more power will be suspended.

‘ WARNING: the Crisis module can be set to inhibit synchronization!

Operating systems
Mobile: Android, BlackBerry, iOS, Symbian, WinMobile

Mi" Minimum required battery percentage. Percentage over this limit trigger an event.
