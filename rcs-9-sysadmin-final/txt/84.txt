Step

Action

Injector section, selectthe Network

3. In the Network Injector tab, click Conﬁg:
synchronization is enabled.

4. During synchronization, RC5 queries
Network Injector every 30 seconds. A
message appears at the end of the ﬁrst
interval requesting consentto install.

NOTE: ifthe update isnotinstalled,
an installation authorization request
will appear the next time Tactical
Control Center is booted.
