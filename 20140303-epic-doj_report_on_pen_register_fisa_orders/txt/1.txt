THE ATTORNEY GENERAL'S REPORT ON THE USE
OF PEN REGISTERS AND TRAP AND TRACE DEVICES
UNDER THE FOREIGN INTELLIGENCE SURVEILLANCE ACT

April 2001

This report is submitted pursuant to the Foreign
Intelligence Surveillance Act of 1978 (the "Act"), 50 U.S.C.
§§ 1801—1811, 1841—1846, as amended, by Pub. L. No. 105—272, 112
Stat. 2404—2413 (1998), and covers all applications to use pen
registers and trap and trace devices conducted under the Act
during the period July 1, 2000 through December 31, 2000. (U)

The Government filed five applications with the Foreign
Intelligence Surveillance Court (FISC) seeking authorization to
use pen registers and/or trap and trace devices during this
period. The Court authorized the use of the pen registers and/or
trap and trace devices as requested in all five applications.
