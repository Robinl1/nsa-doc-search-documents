December 2007

This report is submitted pursuant to the Foreign
Intelligence Surveillance Act of 1978 (the "Act”), 50 U.S.C.
§§ 1841-1846, as amended, and covers all applications to use pen
register and/or trap and trace devices conducted under the Act
during the period January 1, 2007, through June 30, 2007. (U)

applications with the Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Court
(FESC) seeking authorization to use pen register and/or trap and
trace devices during this period. The FISC modified the
proposed order(s) submitted with 10 of the 117 applications that
