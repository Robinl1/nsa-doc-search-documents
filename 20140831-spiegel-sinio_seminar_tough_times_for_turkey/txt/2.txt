suspicion. The strategic relationship with the 1.1.8. is seriously weakened, so the
US. is unlikely to champion Turkey's cause in Europe or with the IMFfWorld Bank
as fervently In addition, Turkey's regional influence, particularly in northern Iraq,

major differences in expectations, perceptions, threat definition, decisionmaking,
and communication had on the strategic relationship and assess the likely
long-term impact.
