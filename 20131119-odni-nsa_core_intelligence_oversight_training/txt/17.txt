services of common concern for the Intelligence Community as
designated by the Director in accordance with this order;

(e) Conduct research, development, and procurement of
technical systems and devices relating to authorized functions
and missions or the provision of services of common concern for
the Intelligence Community;

(f) Protect the security of intelligence related
